Hobby

My hobby is drawing.I love drawing because it makes me feel better. I often get tensed about my school work and it help me calm down.A lot of my free time is spent drawing.When you want to find a way to take a break from distressing thoughts, drawing offers a way to focus your attention on something calming. Sketching, doodling, or coloring provides a way to ground yourself and get some peace from racing thoughts.


"Creativity in and of itself is important for remaining healthy, remaining connected to yourself and connected to the world," says Christianne Strang.


"Anything that engages your creative mind — the ability to make connections between unrelated things and imagine new ways to communicate — is good for you," says Girija Kaimal.
